SMS Terms and Conditions

Last Updated: January 2026

By providing your phone number and opting in, you agree to receive SMS messages from Sean Dinwiddie for customer support and service updates.

1. Program Description

Messages are sent to provide 1-on-1 customer care, including response to inquiries, appointment reminders, and status updates regarding professional software services.

2. Opt-In Methods

You may opt-in to this service through the following methods:

  • Verbal Consent: During a support call, you may be asked if you'd like to receive follow-up information via text. Your verbal "Yes" is recorded in our CRM system.
  • Inquiry Form: Selecting the SMS opt-in checkbox on digital contact forms.

3. Message Frequency

Message frequency varies based on your interactions. We only send messages relevant to your active support requests or inquiries.

4. Cost

Message and data rates may apply. Check with your mobile carrier for details.

5. Opt-Out and Help

You can cancel the SMS service at any time. Just text "STOP" to our number. After you send the SMS message "STOP" to us, we will send you an SMS message to confirm that you have been unsubscribed. After this, you will no longer receive SMS messages from us.

For help, text "HELP" or email notifications@seandinwiddie.com.

6. Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important. We do not share, sell, or rent your phone number or personal data to third parties for marketing or promotional purposes. All information collected is used strictly for providing requested customer support.
